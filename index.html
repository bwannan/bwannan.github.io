More about me

I'm enrolled in Honours Mathematics at the University of Waterloo. I've always enjoyed messing around and making my own projects, some of which are documented on this site.

 

Whenever I can find a break from studying, I enjoy exploring parks and nature. In the summer I usually make time to go on a canoe trip or two; Once I did a 35-day trip down the Missinaibi river.

 

Being a two-time camp counselor, I have lots of experiences and stories to share. I'm always looking to better myself and recently that means learning new skills; This website was my first attempt at using HTML5/CSS/Bootstrap and though it isn't perfect, it has gotten me interested in web design on a more professional level.

 

In addition to my technical skills, I have been a part of Scouts Canada for 8 years now, and have earned the two highest awards possible at my level: Chief Scout and Queen's Venturer.

 

If you've read this far, I'm sure I'd love to meet you! Feel free to contact me however is convenient for you.
